How to Create Alternative Links

Signing Up:

(Skip if you have an account)

1. Open this

2. Click Sign up Free

3. Enter your email

4. Enter a fake name

5. Create a Password and UserID

6. Complete the captcha

7. Agree to the Terms and Conditions

8. Click Send activation email

9. Check your email and click the link that FreeDNS has sent


Creating Them:

1. Now make sure you are on the subdomain page by clicking Subdomains on the left

2. Click Add a Subdomain

3. Click the dropdown next to "Domain:"

4. Choose a domain, or if you want other options, click "Many many more available ..."

5. Click "Shared Domain Registry"

6. Choose a public domain that has unblocked subdomains. For example, if you are choosing the domain "farted.net" then check if this is blocked: "putahythinghereitdoesntmatter.farted.net". If it is blocked, choose an unblocked domain

7. Now enter a desired subdomain next to "Subdomain:". For example, if your domain is mooo.info, and your subdomain is cows, then the link will be cows.mooo.info

8. For "Destination:" choose which proxy link you want to create and enter the corresponding DNS:

9. Now complete the captcha and click Save

The link now should work in about 5 minutes, but sometimes it will take up to an hour
